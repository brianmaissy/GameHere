How to Use Your Phone As A Controller

Tap on item to use.

You can only move perpendicular to the direction you are going.

Swipe up/down and left/right in the dotted box to change the direction of the snake.

Drag each item to the trashcan to discard.

If you have too many items, scroll to view more.
